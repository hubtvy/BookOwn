Situated within 1.8 km of 1st Avenue Penang, Victoria Garden Hotel in George Town features a terrace and rooms with free WiFi. Boasting a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a restaurant. The property is non-smoking and is located 2.1 km from Rainbow Skywalk at Komtar.

All units are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a kettle, a bidet, free toiletries and a desk. Every room has a private bathroom and a hair dryer, and some rooms are equipped with a balcony. Guest rooms at the hotel include air conditioning and a wardrobe.
